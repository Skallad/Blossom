Blossom and birth flowers

The meanings of different flowers have been around for thousands of years. The birth flower on the other hand, though connected to flower symbolism and meaning, represent the different months of the year.

Birth flowers are believed to have been around since Roman times, and tradition in different cultures have carried the practice on through history to this day. Quite not as common as birth stones, they can still make for a nice accompaniment to birthday gifts!

This site offers you to find out what your birth flower is. Head on over to the birth flower tab and click on the month to explore further the meaning of each birth flower!
